Bio

I am an Associate Professor at the China Center for Behavioral Economics and Finance at Southwestern University of Finance and Economics. I obtained my Ph.D. from the Department of Economics, Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University in 2019.

My research tries to bring insights from psychology to bear on topics in microeconomics.
